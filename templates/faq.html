Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about NewsGuard AI and our services

General Questions

Learn more about how NewsGuard AI works and how it can help you verify news

What is NewsGuard AI?

NewsGuard AI is an advanced platform that uses artificial intelligence to verify the authenticity of news articles. Our system analyzes content, cross-references with trusted sources, and provides a comprehensive authenticity score to help users identify reliable information.

How accurate is NewsGuard AI?

Our AI system has been trained on thousands of verified news articles and continuously improves its accuracy. While no system is perfect, NewsGuard AI typically achieves over 90% accuracy in identifying misinformation and fake news when compared to human fact-checkers.

Is NewsGuard AI free to use?

Yes, the basic version of NewsGuard AI is free for individual users. We also offer premium plans with additional features for journalists, organizations, and educational institutions that require more comprehensive analysis tools.

How does NewsGuard AI analyze news articles?

Our system uses a multi-layered approach: First, it extracts key information from the article. Then, it cross-references this information with trusted sources. Finally, it evaluates factors like factual accuracy, source consistency, detail accuracy, and contextual relevance to generate an authenticity score.

Technical Questions

Understand the technology behind our news verification system

What AI models power NewsGuard AI?

NewsGuard AI uses a combination of large language models (LLMs) and custom-trained neural networks specifically designed for news content analysis. Our system incorporates natural language processing, entity recognition, and sentiment analysis to evaluate news authenticity.

Can NewsGuard AI analyze content in languages other than English?

Currently, NewsGuard AI primarily supports English content, with limited capabilities in Spanish and French. We're actively working on expanding our language support to include more global languages in future updates.

How does NewsGuard AI handle paywalled content?

NewsGuard AI cannot directly analyze content behind paywalls. However, it can still provide partial analysis based on publicly available information about the article, including headlines, excerpts, and metadata.

Is there an API available for developers?

Yes, we offer a RESTful API for developers who want to integrate NewsGuard AI's verification capabilities into their own applications. Contact our team for API documentation and access credentials.

Usage & Support

Get help with using NewsGuard AI and learn about our support options

How can I report an incorrect analysis?

If you believe our system has made an error in analyzing an article, you can submit a review request through our contact form. Our team will manually review the article and adjust our algorithms if necessary.

Do you offer educational resources about misinformation?

Yes, we provide educational resources about digital literacy, fact-checking techniques, and recognizing misinformation. These resources are available in our Resources section and are regularly updated with new content.

Can I use NewsGuard AI for research purposes?

Absolutely! NewsGuard AI is used by researchers, journalists, and educators worldwide. For academic or large-scale research projects, please contact us to discuss your specific needs and potential collaboration opportunities.

How can I get technical support?

For technical support, you can reach our team through the contact form on our website. We typically respond within 24 hours during business days. For urgent matters, premium users have access to priority support channels.
