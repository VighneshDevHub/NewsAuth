Getting Started

Welcome to NewsGuard AI documentation! This guide will help you understand how to use our platform to verify news articles and identify potential misinformation.

What is NewsGuard AI?

NewsGuard AI is an advanced platform that uses artificial intelligence to analyze news articles and determine their authenticity. Our system evaluates content based on multiple factors including factual accuracy, source credibility, and contextual relevance.

Creating an Account

To get started with NewsGuard AI:

  1. Visit the homepage and click on "Sign Up"
  2. Enter your email address and create a password
  3. Verify your email address through the confirmation link
  4. Complete your profile information
Note: Basic accounts are free and provide access to essential verification features. Premium accounts offer additional capabilities for journalists and organizations.

Using the Platform

Verifying a News Article

To verify a news article:

  1. Copy the text of the article you want to verify
  2. Paste it into the verification box on the homepage
  3. Click "Verify Now" to begin the analysis process
  4. Wait for the system to process the article (typically 10-15 seconds)
  5. Review the authenticity analysis and detailed breakdown

Interpreting Results

The verification results include:

  • Authenticity Score: An overall rating from 0-100
  • Key Findings: Major observations about the article's accuracy
  • Supporting Evidence: Quotes from trusted sources that support or contradict the article
  • Score Breakdown: Detailed scores across multiple evaluation criteria
Important: NewsGuard AI is designed to assist in evaluating news, but should not be the sole determinant of an article's credibility. Always use critical thinking skills and consult multiple sources.

Understanding Scores

NewsGuard AI evaluates articles across four key dimensions:

Factual Accuracy (0-40 points)

Measures how well the article's claims align with verified facts from trusted sources. Articles with higher factual accuracy scores contain information that can be independently verified through multiple reliable sources.

Source Consistency (0-30 points)

Evaluates how consistently the article's narrative aligns with reporting from established news sources. Higher scores indicate that the article's perspective is consistent with mainstream reporting on the topic.

Detail Accuracy (0-20 points)

Assesses the precision of specific details mentioned in the article, such as names, dates, locations, and statistics. Articles with higher detail accuracy scores provide specific information that matches verified data.

Contextual Relevance (0-10 points)

Measures how well the article places information in proper context. Higher scores indicate that the article provides appropriate background and avoids misleading framing of events.

Overall Authenticity Score

The combined score (0-100) represents the overall assessment of the article's authenticity:

  • 90-100: Highly Authentic - Very reliable information
  • 70-89: Mostly Authentic - Generally reliable with minor issues
  • 50-69: Somewhat Authentic - Contains a mix of accurate and questionable content
  • 30-49: Questionable - Contains significant inaccuracies or misleading information
  • 0-29: Likely Inauthentic - Contains major factual errors or fabricated content

  • Generate organization-specific reporting templates

Troubleshooting

Common Issues

Issue: Analysis takes too long to complete

Solution: Try breaking down very long articles into smaller sections. For articles over 5,000 words, our system may require additional processing time. If the issue persists, try refreshing the page and submitting again.

Issue: Low scores for accurate articles

Solution: Check if the article contains very recent information that may not yet be widely reported by other sources. Our system continuously updates its knowledge base, but there may be a short delay for breaking news. You can also try providing additional context in your submission.

Issue: Unable to access premium features

Solution: Verify that your account has an active premium subscription. Navigate to Account Settings > Subscription to check your current plan. If you believe there's an error, please contact our support team.

Getting Support

If you encounter any issues not covered in this documentation:

  • Contact our support team through the Contact page

Premium users have access to priority support with faster response times.
